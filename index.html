Fine Artist

From drawing Simba in red Crayola, to my AP Studio Senior Thesis, to graduating with a BFA in Painting, fine art takes precendence in my history as an artist. Digital Designer

After landing a role in marketing, my eyes were opened to a world in which I could combine my skills as a fine artist, with my interest in technology and my passion for storytelling. My focus is on idea generation, content creation, and creating aesthetic experiences that tell a story and inspire the imagination.
